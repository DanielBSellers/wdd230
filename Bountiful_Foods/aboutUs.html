Shop Local

At Bountiful Foods we beleive it's important to shop local to keep money in the community and to help the economy get stronger for those who vist our great town. That's why a vast majority of the food we sell is grown locally and why we only sell the best organic food!

Why Carlsbad?

Carlsbad is home to multiple beaches, the famous LEGOLAND™, and of course amazing food! Our shop can make a good day into a great day by tryin one of our organic, healthy, and very tastey smoothies or another one of our locally sourced produce.

Food Trucks

We understand it can be hard to fight traffic on Avenida Encinas, leave the office for lunch, and try and move a large order for your Neighborhood block party. That's why we have our food trucks come to you anywhere along the coastal areas of Los Angeles to San Diego!
