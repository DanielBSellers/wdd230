Who We Are!

Here we produce organic, locally grown foods for the heath and satisfaction of our community. Our mission is to provide fresh and healthy food options, including organic, made to order, fruit drinks and fruit bags, to the local population and to tourists visiting the coastal areas.

Where We Are!

We are located right next to Carlsbad State Beach. It offers swimming, surfing, scuba diving, fishing and beachcombing. This small beach is located at the foot of coastal bluffs, south of the town on Carlsbad.
