Description of Target Audience

My target audience are buisness owners who are trying to strengthen there there presence in the community and those who are trying to start there own buisness and want to gain viable buisness contacts.

  1. Mike just graduated from Weber State University with a masters in buisness administartion and wants to start a fast food joint. Mike checks his local chamber of commerse website to connect with other small buisness oweners that may be able to help him get a jump start on his own buisness.

He jumps on the chamber of commerse for that city to see if there is anything on there about already established real estate experience on there.

  5. Betty has worked for Intermoutain Healthcare for the last fourty years as an upper manager and is looking to retire. She decides to check the chamber of commerse site to see if she can connect with anyone who will take care of her work place the same way she does.
