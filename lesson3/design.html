Design Choices


Color Schemea

I chose this color schemea because my goal is to have the site be recognized as something that belongs in my hometown South Ogden Utah. Two main things that South Ogden is recognized for is Weber State University and for being nestled against the Wasatch moutains. I feel the colors I chose are easy to assosite with those two things.


Typography

I chose this font because it reminded me of the font that is used by Weber State University. It is also easy to read to those who would choose to visit the site.
