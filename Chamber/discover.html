South Ogden Utah Chamber of Commerce

,

While recently renovated in the early 2020's the 40th street park has been a staple of South Ogden for years! Nestled between the police station and the Wasatch Christian Early Learning Center it provides a safe and exciting experiences during the warmer months of the year and is great for the whole family!

The Club Heights park is a staple in the childhood of many youth and adults alike who grew up in the South Ogden area. It's sourounded by a bunch of trees that match the aesthetic of the structure itself. It's been a great spot for many elementary school student and families and continues to be so.

Glassman park is a perfect site to build and strengthen a community and has been for quite some time. It has a great parking lot, facilites to hold out door meetings, a large spacious field, and is located near residences and other community gathering spots.

The South Ogden Nature park is the perfect for a memorable community experience regardless of the occasion. It has pavilions to sit and discuss as well as a splash pad to provide fun for all ages. The highlight of this area is the nature trail that could be used as a nature experience or a great workout.

South Ogden Jr. Hight school opened after renovations for in the 2011-2012 school year. It still stands as the primary jr. high school for the students in that area. The school is equiped with all the sports facilities a school could ask for and has a reputation of being a place that provides a education while shaping morally responsible students.
